Heterochronous Dataflow Domain

The Heterochronous Dataflow (HDF) Domain, created by Ye Zhou is an optimization of the SDFDirector and implements the HDF model of computation. The HDF model of computation is a generalization of Synchronous Dataflow (SDF). In SDF, the set of port rates of an actor (called rate signatures) are constant. In HDF, however, rate signatures are allowed to change between iterations of the HDF schedule. Within each state, the HDF model behaves like an SDF model.
