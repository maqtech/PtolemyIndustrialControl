CT domain demo - Square Wave Response

Introduction

This applet demonstrates the square wave response of a second order linear continuous time system.

The system is given by: 

    dx1/dt = x2
    dx2/dt = 1/m(-k*x1 - b*x2 + u)
where, m = 500, k = 25, b = 2500, and u is the square wave with amplitude +/- 2.0.

You may choose different ODE solvers to see the change in the solution.
