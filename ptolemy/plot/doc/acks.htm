Sponsors

  • Agilent Technologies
  • Cadence Design Systems
  • Hitachi
  • Hughes Space and Communications (now Boeing)
  • Philips
and the following organizations:
  • State of California MICRO Program
  • GSRC (Gigascale Silicon Research Center), which is funded by
    • Semiconductor Industry Association (SIA)
    • SEMI/SEMATECH
    • MARCO (Microelectronics Advanced Research Corporation)
