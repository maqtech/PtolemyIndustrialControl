Sticky Ball: A Hybrid System

This applet models two (sticky) balls on a flat table. Each of them are attached to a spring and the surface of the table is assumed to be smooth. So if both balls are free, they will keep swing back and forth. However, if the two balls hit each other, they will stick together. The stickiness is assumed to be exponentially decaying after they hit. So after a while, they will separate. To run the applet, click Go
