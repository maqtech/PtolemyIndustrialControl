Sketch

Using the right mouse button, sketch a signal below. The signal is multiplied by a sine wave.

Notice that you can zoom in with the left mouse button and very precisely control the sketched curve. You can also update just portion of the curve by sketching over only a subset of the horizontal axis. You can undo your updates by typing Control-Z (the undo history is infinite). You can redo what you have undone by typing Control-Y. For undo and redo to work, you have to make sure the focus is in the plot window (clicking on the fill button will do this).
